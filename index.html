FROM THE FOUNDER

Why I built Pickie.

My first pickleball game was August 2024 in Ho Chi Minh City. A friend dragged me along, and I was hooked before it ended.

Since then I've played tournaments across cities and countries, and partnered with more players than I can count. The same struggle followed me everywhere: finding a partner you truly click with is hard. Not just a similar level. Real chemistry, the kind that makes a game flow.

So I turned everything those matches taught me into Pickie's chemistry algorithm, built for players who want more partners who fit and for anyone just picking up a paddle. Whether you play for the social side, for tournaments, or just for fun, Pickie helps you connect with the people who fit your game.

"Pickleball should be fun. Playing with people is fun. Playing with the right people, with the right chemistry? Even more fun."

Kevin's hope is bigger than one app: grow pickleball in Indonesia, Vietnam, across Southeast Asia, and everywhere a net goes up.
