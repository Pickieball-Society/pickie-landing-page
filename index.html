FROM THE FOUNDER

Why I built Pickie.

I hit my first pickleball in August 2024 in Ho Chi Minh City — a friend brought me along. I was hooked before the first game ended.

Since then I've played tournaments across cities and countries, and partnered with more players than I can count. The same struggle followed me everywhere: finding a partner you truly click with is hard. Not just a similar level — real chemistry, the kind that makes a game flow.

So I turned everything those matches taught me into Pickie's chemistry algorithm — for players who want more partners that fit, and for those just starting out. Whether you play for the social side, for tournaments, or just for fun, Pickie helps you connect with the people who fit your game.

"Pickleball must be fun. Playing with people is fun — playing with the right people, with the right chemistry, is even more fun."

Kevin's hope is bigger than an app: to help pickleball grow — in Indonesia, in Vietnam, across Southeast Asia, and beyond.
